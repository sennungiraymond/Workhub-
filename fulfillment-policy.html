Fulfillment Policy

This Fulfillment Policy outlines the terms, conditions, and procedures governing the services provided by WorkHub. By using the WorkHub platform, users agree to comply with the policies described below.

1. Scope of Services

WorkHub is a digital job portal that connects job seekers and employers. The platform provides services such as job postings, job applications, employer branding, recruitment tools, and related digital services.

All services provided on WorkHub are offered as part of a frontend prototype for demonstration purposes and may be improved or expanded in future development phases.

2. Service Delivery

Service delivery on WorkHub is primarily digital and occurs immediately after user actions. Examples include:

  • Access to job listings after account registration
  • Submission of job applications through the platform
  • Activation of employer dashboards and recruitment tools
  • Display of featured or promoted job listings (where applicable)

3. Timelines

Most services on WorkHub are delivered instantly or within a short time frame. Typical timelines include:

  • Account creation: Immediate
  • Job posting publication: Immediate or within 24 hours
  • Application submission confirmation: Immediate
  • Employer access to applicants: Immediate after application submission

Any delays caused by technical issues will be communicated to users where possible.

4. Payments, Pricing, and Billing

WorkHub may offer paid services such as featured job listings, premium employer accounts, or promotional services. Pricing details and payment providers will be introduced and clearly communicated at a later stage.

Payment systems, including mobile money options, are implemented as a prototype and do not process real financial transactions at this stage.

5. Refunds and Cancellations

Since WorkHub services are digital, refunds and cancellations are handled under the following conditions:

  • Refunds may be considered if a paid service fails to activate due to system errors.
  • No refunds will be issued for services that have already been fully delivered.
  • Users may request cancellation of future services before activation.

All refund or cancellation requests must be submitted through the official WorkHub contact channels.

6. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for providing accurate information on their profiles, job postings, and applications. WorkHub is not responsible for:

  • False or misleading job listings
  • Incorrect applicant information
  • Employment decisions made by employers

7. Limitation of Liability

WorkHub acts as a connection platform and does not guarantee employment outcomes or hiring success. The platform is not liable for disputes between job seekers and employers.

8. Policy Updates

WorkHub reserves the right to update or modify this Fulfillment Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes where applicable.

9. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Fulfillment Policy, users may contact WorkHub through the official Contact Us page.
