Features for Job Seekers

WorkHub provides powerful tools to help job seekers find and apply for opportunities easily and efficiently.

Advanced Job Search & Filters

Search jobs using keywords, location, salary range, job type, and experience level for accurate results.

One-Click Job Application

Apply for jobs directly on the platform using a simplified application process.

Job Alerts & Notifications

Receive alerts and notifications based on saved searches and user preferences.

Profile & Resume Management

Create a digital profile, upload resumes (PDF or DOCX), and manage multiple documents.

Application Tracking

Track the status of submitted job applications including viewed, shortlisted, or rejected.

Social Sign-In

Sign up or log in quickly using social platforms such as Google, LinkedIn, or Facebook.

Features for Employers

Employers are provided with professional tools to manage recruitment and hiring processes efficiently.

Job Posting & Management

Create, edit, and manage job listings easily from an employer dashboard.

Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

Manage candidates using a Kanban-style dashboard and track hiring stages.

Resume Search Database

Search and filter candidate profiles based on skills, experience, and qualifications.

Employer Branding

Customize company profiles with logos, descriptions, benefits, and media content.

Interview Scheduling

Schedule interviews with candidates directly within the platform.

Technical & Administrative Features

WorkHub is built with modern technologies to ensure security, performance, and scalability.

Mobile Responsive Design

Fully responsive interface that works seamlessly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops.

AI-Based Matching (Simulated)

Intelligent matching logic suggests relevant jobs and candidates based on profiles.

Secure Data Handling

Implements secure design practices, data protection principles, and GDPR awareness.

SEO Optimization

Search engine friendly structure to improve job visibility on platforms like Google Jobs.

Payment System (Mobile Money – Prototype)

Integrated mobile money payment interface with future-ready pricing and providers.

Analytics & Reporting

Dashboards for tracking job views, applications, and platform performance.
